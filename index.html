Nelson Mandela

Introduction

Nelson Mandela was a South African anti-apartheid revolutionary, a political leader, and an extraordinary philanthropist...

Early Activism

  • Joined the African National Congress (ANC) in 1943
  • Led campaigns of peaceful defiance against discriminatory laws
  • Co-founded the ANC Youth League

Imprisonment and Struggle

  • Arrested multiple times
  • Sentenced to life imprisonment in 1964
  • Became a global symbol of resistance against apartheid

Presidency and Reconciliation

  • Released from prison in 1990
  • Elected as South Africa's first black president in 1994
  • Established the Truth and Reconciliation Commission
